St Vincent's feast

28-9-2014 - after 10.30 mass

On Sunday, September 28th, after 10.30 mass, we will celebrate the feast of St Vincent.

After the two eucharistic celebrations at 8.30 and 10.30 in honor of St Vincent we will have a Buffet Lunch with the food provided by the generosity of all.

Tickets are 20 HK$ and can be bought after each mass.

Our Church feast should become a feast of abundance for all. For this reason please share a dish with all the community.
