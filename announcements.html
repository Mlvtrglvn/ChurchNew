Announcements

Last update: 31-1-2016

Books / Clothes Sale
Thanks very much to all those who donated items to or helped organize the sale of books and clothes, as well as to those who bought items. $3560 was raised by the sale for Caritas-Nepal.
Special Collection
Next weekend, 6th-7th February, we will have our final special collection to conclude our Advent Action for the post-earthquake rebuilding in Nuwakot District being organized by Caritas—Nepal. Please give generously.
Palms Collection
Until Monday, February 8th, we will collect blessed palms and branches from last year to prepare ashes for Ash Wednesday. Please leave them in the sacristy.
Ash Wednesday
Mass will be celebrated and ashes distributed at St. Vincent’s at 8 pm on Ash Wednesday, February 10th. Please be reminded that Ash Wednesday is a day of fasting and abstinence.
Jubilee Year of Mercy Pilgrimage
Sunday, February 7th is the deadline to register for the pilgrimage to the Jubilee church of St. Joseph’s on Yim Tin Tsai 鹽田仔 island in Sai Kung on Sunday, February 21. Please sign up on the bulletin board. We will meet at the Sai Kung pier at 10:30am. Mass will be celebrated at St. Joseph’s chapel at 11:30; please bring a picnic lunch. The pilgrimage will end about 2:30pm. Tickets will be $50/person to cover the cost of the boat and a donation to St. Joseph’s.
Chinese New Year Mass
Mass will be said at St. Vincent’s on the morning of Monday, February 8 th, at 8:30 am, the first day of the Chinese New Year.
Coffee Morning
Coffee morning after 10:30 Mass each week, and on the first Sunday of the month after 8:30 Mass, provide a chance for parishioners to get to know each other and share fellowship. If you enjoy these occasions, please take a turn to provide and serve snacks and drinks. Sign up sheets are on the bulletin board. Thank you!
Advent Action: Caritas-Nepal
Each winter, in honor of our patron saint, the St. Vincent’s community raises funds from the beginning of Advent through Chinese New Year for a charity that serves the very poor or marginalized. This year we will support the earthquake relief and community rebuilding organized by Caritas—Nepal. More information about the charity and the progress of Advent Action will be available on the bulletin board. Anyone interested in helping is very welcome—please speak to any member of the pastoral team or church council to offer ideas or time. Your generosity allows us each year to share some of the joy of the Advent, Christmas and New Year seasons with those less fortunate.
