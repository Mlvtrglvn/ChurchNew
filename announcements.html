Announcements

Last update: 10-5-2014

Second Collection for Holy Spirit Seminary
There will be a second collection in support of the diocesan Holy Spirit Seminary Sunday, May 11. Cheques should be made payable to "The Catholic Diocese of Hong Kong".
Easter Cup 復活盃
The exercise group at St. Andrew’s is organizing an “Easter Cup” on Sunday, May 18th, from 2 to 4:30 pm at Catholic Ming Yuen Secondary School 天主教鳴遠中學, located on Hau Tak Estate 厚德村 in Hang Hau, a few minutes walk from the MTR. Activities will include 3-on-3 basketball, a sports area, and parent-child games. Everyone in the parish community is welcome.

Church Planting Project
The Catholic Messengers of Green Consciousness invite us to join their “Church Planting Project”. A bilingual (English/Cantonese) “farmer” from the Catholic Messengers will hold two sessions from 12 noon to 1:00 pm on Sunday, 25 May and Sunday, 1 June on growing and monitoring the plants, which may be used for decorating the church or our own homes. If you are interested in participating, please see the information posted on the bulletin board, or contact Ellen (10:30 Mass) or Edith (8:30 Mass).
Adoration of the Holy Sacrament
The Adoration of the Holy Sacrament will be held regularly on the afternoon of the last Sunday of each month.
Fr. George Away
From 28 April until 17 May, Fr. George will accompany a delegation to Europe to visit the tomb of the last missionary bishop of Hong Kong, Lorenzo Bianchi, and to participate in a celebration of remembrance for the canonization of Popes John XXIII and John Paul II. In his absence, his duties will be covered by other members of the pastoral team.
Renovations of Church Hall
The preparations for renovation of the church hall downstairs have begun. We appreciate your patience with any inconvenience or disorder during the process of improvements to our building.
Happy Mother’s Day!
The pastoral team and church council wish all of you and your families a joyful and blessed Mother’s Day.
