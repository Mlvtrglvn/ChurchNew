Announcements

Last update: 26-4-2014

Enrollment for Confirmation of Young People, Rite of the Christian Initiation for Adults, and First Communion
Applications will be accepted until April 27 for young people 14 and older who wish to be confirmed; for adults interested in the Rite of the Christian Initiation for Adults; and for children 7 and older who would like to make their First Communion. Children should have regularly attended Service of the Word for at least six months before the start of the Communion catechesis. For enrollment forms or more information, please speak to Fr. George.
Adoration of the Holy Sacrament
All are invited to attend the Adoration of the Holy Sacrament Sunday, April 27, at 3:00 pm. The Adoration will be held regularly on the afternoon of the last Sunday of each month.
Cathedral Mass to Honour Canonization
A concelebrate solemn Mass in honour of the canonization of Popes John XXIII and John Paul II will be held in the Cathedral, Caine Road, today at 6:00pm, with Cardinal John Tong presiding. All clergy and laity are welcome to participate.
Music Seminar and Adult Catechesis
The Chaplaincy for Filipino Migrants will hold a music seminar and adult catechesis on May 1st from 9 am to 5 pm. Everyone is invited. For more information, please call Vicky at 9518 1462
Fr. George Away
From 28 April until 17 May, Fr. George will accompany a delegation to Europe to visit the tomb of the last missionary bishop of Hong Kong, Lorenzo Bianchi, and to participate in a celebration of remembrance for the canonization of Popes John XXIII and John Paul II. In his absence, his duties will be covered by other members of the pastoral team.
Renovations of Church Hall
The preparations for renovation of the church hall downstairs have begun. We appreciate your patience with any inconvenience or disorder during the process of improvements to our building.
Happy Easter and Thank You!
The pastoral team and church council wish all of you and your families and friends a very happy and blessed Easter. We would particularly like to express our gratitude to all those who contributed their time and effort to the Holy Week celebrations.
