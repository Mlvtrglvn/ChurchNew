Announcements

Last update: 21-6-2015

First Holy Communion
Formation for First Holy Communion will begin on September 13th. Children who are at least 7 years old and would like to join the formation should register in the sacristy.
Confirmation
Confirmation preparation will resume on Sept. 6 after 10:30 Mass. There will be a meeting with the parents of confirmation candidates that day; please prepare the requested documents for the sponsors.
Sunday School Catechists
There will be a meeting of Sunday school catechists on Sunday, Aug. 30th, after 10:30 Mass. Anyone interested in helping with catechesis, please speak with Sister Teresa.
Rite of Christian Initiation of Adults
The first meeting for new catechumens and those interested in receiving the sacraments of Christian initiation will be held on Sunday, August 30th, after 10:30 Mass. Please inform family members, friends, students and others who could be interested.
PCGHK All-in-one Seminar
On Sunday, August 30th from 1 to 5 pm, an official from the Philippine Consulate General will come to St. Vincent’s to give an all-in-one seminar on Hong Kong immigration and labor law updates and a Post-Arrival Orientation Seminar. Officers from SSS, PAG-IBIG, and PHILHEALTH, as well as those in charge of registration and updating of OFW voting rights, will attend and answer questions. A notice is posted on the bulletin board at the back of the church. Everyone is welcome.
Holympics
The 3rd Holympics will be held on Monday, September 28th (a public holiday) from 9am to 6pm at Sing Yin Secondary School 聖言中學 in Choi Hung. Activities include dodgeball, pick-up basketball, and various water games and mixers. Young people (under 35) may register online register online here; early-bird registration (before September 7th) is $30 and includes lunch.
World Youth Day
With the consent of our bishop, St. Andrew’s and Tai Po parishes will together organize one of the diocesan groups to participate in World Youth Day in Krakow (Poland) in July 2016. Only 20 young people (16-35 years old) from each parish are permitted and will include both English- and Chinese-speaking youth. The deadline for registration is October 18th (Mission Sunday); enrollment forms and detailed information are available in the sacristy. Father Fabio and Father George will be in charge of our parish group.
