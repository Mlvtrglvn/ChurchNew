Announcements

Last update: 28-3-2014

Holy Week Schedule
Holy Thursday: The Mass of the Lord’s Supper will be held at 8 pm. Adoration of the Eucharist (Gethsemani Hour) will continue after Mass until 11 pm.

Good Friday: Stations of the Cross will be at 3 pm. From 4 to 5:30 pm, a meditation upon the last seven words of Christ will be held. Confessions will also be heard. At 6 pm will be the Celebration of the Passion of the Lord. The church will remain open until 9 pm for individual adoration and confession. Please note that Good Friday is a day of fasting and abstinence from meat.

Holy Saturday: Easter Vigil Mass at 8 pm. Easter Sunday: Mass at 8:30 and 10:30 am. Children are invited to bring Easter baskets with Easter foods for blessing at Mass. An explanation of this tradition and suggestions for basket preparation are posted at the back of the church.

Lenten Theme
The theme of this year’s diocesan Lenten campaign from Ash Wednesday to Easter is “Faith comes to perfection with love”. Each week will also have a sub-theme to encourage us to participate through prayer, reflection and sacrifice.
Altar Server meeting
There will be a meeting April 13, Sunday, after 10:30 Mass for all altar servers from both Masses to prepare the Paschal vigil.
Easter Egg Decoration
There will be Easter egg decoration April 13, Sunday, after the 10:30 Mass. Everyone is welcome.
Preparation of Church for Easter
Volunteers are needed on Holy Saturday morning between 10:30 and 12:30 to help clean up around the church and help decorate for Easter. There is a sign up sheet on the bulletin board at the back of the church. Your help is very much appreciated.
Diocesan Lenten Campaign
The housewares sale raised $2360 towards the Lenten campaign. Thank you for your generosity in supporting the campaign. We would also like to express our gratitude to the Vogee family for donating the items and to the Friendship Choir for organizing and running the sale.

Lenten Campaign boxes and envelopes will be collected on Good Friday, April 18. They are available at the back of the church.
Blessing of the Easter Basket Foods
All families are encouraged to prepare an Easter basket with Easter foods to be blessed during the Easter Masses. An explanation of this tradition and suggestions for basket preparation are available at the back of the church.
Renovations of Church Hall
The preparations for renovation of the church hall downstairs have begun. We appreciate your patience with any inconvenience or disorder during the process of improvements to our building.
Palm Sunday of the Passion of the Lord
On Palm Sunday, April 13, both Masses will be preceded by a solemn procession. Please bring palm fronds, green branches, or flowers to express our gratitude to Christ the King. We will also celebrate World Youth Day, as we prepare spiritually for the Sixth Asian Youth Day in South Korea in August, in which our Pope, Francis, will participate. Enrollment for Confirmation of Young People, Rite of the Christian Initiation for Adults, and First Communion
Applications will be accepted until April 27 for young people 14 and older who wish to be confirmed; for adults interested in the Rite of the Christian Initiation for Adults; and for children 7 and older who would like to make their First Communion. Children should have regularly attended Service of the Word for at least six months before the start of the Communion catechesis. For enrollment forms or more information, please speak to Fr. George.
