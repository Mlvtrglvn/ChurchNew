Announcements

Last update: 22-3-2015

Saturday Anticipated Mass
A three-month trial of a Saturday evening anticipated Mass will begin at St. Vincent’s on November 8th. In consultation with the church council, the time for Saturday Mass has been set at 5:30 pm.
Way of the Cross
The Stations of the Cross will be prayed at St. Vincent’s each Friday in Lent at 8 pm. Confessions will also be heard. A plenary indulgence will be granted to all who piously complete this devotion.
DEY Events
Information is posted on the bulletin board about several events in mid- March organized by the Diocesan English-speaking Youth group, including a bicycle pilgrimage and outdoor Way of the Cross.
ACTS Retreats
In conjunction with the Ampleforth Center for Theology and Spirituality and Diocesan English-speaking Youth, Fr. Chad Boulton will hold a series of retreats for teenagers and adults between Thursday, March 26th and Monday, March 30th. The registration deadline is this Monday. Information is posted on the bulletin board and is available on the ACTS website.
Palm Sunday
On Palm Sunday, both Masses will be preceded by a solemn procession. Please bring palm fronds, green branches, or flowers to express our gratitude to Christ the King. Leaflets with the entire Holy Week schedule are available at the back of the church.
Easter Egg Decorating
After 10:30 Mass on Palm Sunday we will have Easter egg decorating. Everyone is welcome. If you can help out, please speak to Lani Nasayao or sign up on the bulletin board.
Shroud of Turin
A talk on the Shroud of Turin will be given by Fr. Andrew Dalton, L. C., at the chapel of Our Lady of Mount Carmel Church in Wanchai on Holy Thursday, April 2nd, from 5 to 6:30 pm. Mass will be at 8 pm.
8:30 Mass Coffee Morning
Due to a lack of volunteers, the coffee morning after 8:30 Mass will be held once a month, on the first Sunday. The next 8:30 coffee morning will be on March 1st. Please contact Marie Hendricks by email Chiliqueen58@gmail.com or phone (9033 2527) if you can help out.
Call for Readers (5:30 Mass)
Anyone willing to serve as a reader at 5:30 Mass, please speak to Fr. George or Ellen McGill after Mass.
