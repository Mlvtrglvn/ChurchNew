Announcements

Last update: 10-1-2015

Saturday Anticipated Mass
A three-month trial of a Saturday evening anticipated Mass will begin at St. Vincent’s on November 8th. In consultation with the church council, the time for Saturday Mass has been set at 5:30 pm.
Lector Formation and Commissioning
The last meeting of the formation for lectors and occasional readers will be on Sunday, January 11th, from 9:30 to 10:15 AM in the former parish office. Candidates for lectorship who have missed previous meetings of the formation should attend a special review class on the evening of Saturday, January 24th, at 6:30 pm. The commissioning of lectors and blessing of occasional readers will be held at 10:30 Mass on January 25th; all candidates please come to the former parish office at 10 am for preparation of the liturgy of commissioning and blessing.
Annual General Meeting
The Annual General Meeting of the St. Vincent’s church council will be held on January 18th after 10:30 Mass. Everyone is encouraged to attend and to offer suggestions for improving our pastoral work. Anyone interested in joining the 2015 church council may obtain a form in the sacristy; please speak to any council member or to any of the priests for more information.
St. Peter’s Pence Collection
It is the practice for Catholics all over the world to make yearly offerings to the Holy Father to help him carry out his manifold services to the universal Church. In our Diocese, a second collection in support of this purpose is to be taken up at all public Masses on Sunday, January 18th. Cheques should be made payable to “The Catholic Diocese of Hong Kong.”
Music Festival
The annual Music Festival will be held on January 25th after the 10:30 Mass. Everyone is welcome to join. If you would like to participate, please write your name on the list at the back of the church on or before 18th of January. Please also bring some snacks or goodies for our Bake Sale on the same day. Each year we have this as part of our Advent Action to raise funds to help underprivileged communities in other parts of the world. We welcome your generosity.
Confirmation
Confirmation classes will begin on February 22, the first Sunday of Lent. If you would like to be confirmed, please speak with Fr. George.
Week of Prayer for Christian Unity
In union with other churches and ecclesial communities, we will jointly celebrate the Week of Prayer for Christian Unity from Sunday, January 18th to January 25th. The theme for this year is: “Give me to drink” (cf. Jn 4:7). At our monthly Adoration of the Holy Sacrament on January 25th, we will pray especially for unity among Christians.
Theology of Family
A course on the Theology of Family is being organized by the Saint John Paul II Formation Centre in Kennedy Town from January 26th to February 1st. Please see the poster on the large bulletin board for more information.
Flowers
Volunteers are very much needed to help with flowers; you can be reimbursed up to $400 if you provide a receipt. Please sign up on the list posted on the small bulletin board at the back of the church.
