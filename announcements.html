Announcements

Last update: 6-8-2014

Assumption of the Blessed Virgin Mary
On Friday, August 15 (the Feast of the Assumption of Our Lady) there will be a Mass at 8 pm and vigil of prayer at 9 pm at St. Vincent’s in unity with Asian Youth Day and the Holy Father’s trip to South Korea.
Episcopal Ordination
The episcopal ordination of the three Auxiliary Bishops-elect of our Diocese, namely, the Most Rev. Michael YEUNG Ming-cheung, Stephen LEE Bun-sang and Joseph HA Chi-shing, OFM, will be held in the Catholic Cathedral, Caine Road, on Saturday, 30 August 2014, at 3:00 p.m., with Cardinal John TONG presiding. The faithful and the clergy are welcome to participate.
Sacraments for the Sick or Disabled
Parishioners who are ill or disabled and wish to regularly receive Holy Communion at home, request Confession or receive the Sacrament of the Anointing of the Sick, should please speak to Fr. George or one of the Extraordinary Ministers of Holy Communion before or after Mass.
Holympics
The Catholic Diocese’s English Youth Community (DEY) is hosting its second sports day, the Holympics, on Tuesday, September 9 from 9:00am to 5:45pm at Sing Yin Secondary School 聖言中學 in Choi Hung. All young people are invited to join for a day of sport, games and fellowship. For more information, please refer to the poster at the back of the church or speak with Fr. George, Ellen McGill (10:30) or Edith To (8:30).
Adoration of the Holy Sacrament
The Adoration of the Holy Sacrament will be held regularly on the afternoon of the last Sunday of each month. We will pray particularly for the fruitful first apostolic travel of the Pope in Asia and for the Hong Kong delegation to Asian Youth Day.
Rosary
After May, we will continue to pray the rosary each Sunday from 10:00 am to 10:20 am. Everyone is invited to join.
Photography group
A few volunteers are needed to take pictures of ceremonies and events at the church. If you might be interested in helping, please speak to Edith To at 8:30 Mass or Gilroy Fernandez at 10:30 Mass.
Flowers
Volunteers are very much needed to help with the flowers each week, and especially in the summer. Please consider taking a turn; there is a sign-up sheet on the bulletin board at the back of the church.
