Announcements

Last update: 30-4-2016

“Visita Iglesia” Pilgrimage
If you have signed up for the pilgrimage next Sunday afternoon, please pay for your tickets after Mass on Sunday, May 1st.
First Communion
Children who are at least 7 years old and would like to receive First Holy Communion should be registered by their parents in the sacristy. A meeting for these parents and children will be held on Sunday, May 8th after 10:30 Mass.
Second Collection
World Communications Day will be celebrated on the Solemnity of the Ascension, 8th May. The main theme is “Communication and Mercy: A Fruitful Encounter”. A second collection will be taken for the promotion of the media apostolate in our diocese. Cheques should be made payable to “The Catholic Diocese of Hong Kong.
Pentecost (15 May)
On Pentecost there will be a special collection before church and after all anticipated and Sunday Masses to support the diocesan delegation to the World Youth Day. After 10:30 Mass, we will celebrate International Day in the church hall. Everyone is encouraged to bring some food from your country to share, to wear your national costume or football colours, and/or to contribute music, dance, poetry, etc. Some of the World Youth Day delegates will also join the performances.
