Holy Week schedule

The schedule is also posted on the bullettin board.
Youth and Reader Formation
On this Sunday, February 28th we will begin
  • 9:45 am: Post-Communion catechesis: For young people who have received First Communion but not yet been confirmed
  • 11:30 am: Post-confirmation / reader catechesis: For young people who have already been confirmed, as well as anyone who would like to become a reader.
To be a reader, you must have been confirmed. The reader-related part of the formation will last until June. All meetings will be in the old parish office. If you have not yet registered for catechesis, or would like to attend in order to become a reader, please speak with Fr. George.
Easter Egg Decorating
There will be Easter egg decorating after 10:30 Mass on Sunday 20th March downstairs in the church hall. Everyone is welcome.
Special Collection
There will be a special collection on Good Friday at the celebration of the Passion of the Lord in support of our brethren in the Holy Land and the numerous pastoral, charitable, educational and social institutions and projects sponsored by the Church there. Any cheques should be made out to “The Catholic Diocese of Hong Kong”.
Easter Decoration
In preparation for Easter Celebration, the decoration of the church will begin at around 10:00am on 26th of March, Holy Saturday. If you can help out, please sign up on the bulletin board. Thank you!
Easter Sunday
Blessing of Easter Foods: Families are invited to bring baskets with Easter foods for blessing at Mass. An explanation of this tradition and suggestions for basket preparation are posted at the back of the church.

Egg Hunt: There will be an egg hunt to the side of the church after both Masses on Easter Sunday. All children are welcome.

Cars: Please note that there will be no parking on the side of the church on Easter Sunday. We apologize for any inconvenience.
Stations of the Cross
The Way of the Cross will be prayed at St. Vincent’s every Friday evening in Lent at 8 pm. A plenary indulgence is granted for those who piously complete this devotion. Confessions will also be heard before and after the meditation.
Lenten Campaign
The purple Paschal Lamb banks and paper collection boxes for the diocesan Lenten Campaign previously given to children will be collected in a special basket on Good Friday and Easter Sunday, along with any other donations.
