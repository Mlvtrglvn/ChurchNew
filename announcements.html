Announcements

Last update: 21-6-2015

Altar Servers
Altar servers or candidates who missed the blessings should please speak to Fr. George or Sr. Teresa. If you would like to become an altar server and have received First Holy Communion, or will receive it this coming year, please speak with Sr. Teresa.
Parish Council Elections
The 13th St. Andrew’s Parish Council elections will be held at the parish annual general meeting on July 12th. Registration to be a candidate for the new council parish council, which will serve from 2015 to 2017, will be accepted after all anticipated and Sunday Masses at St. Andrew’s Church from Saturday, May 9th through Sunday, May 31st.
Adoration of the Blessed Sacrament
Our monthly Adoration will be held on Sunday, June 28th, at 3 pm. In the context of the Solemnity of Saints Paul and Peter, we will pray for our pope and for the spiritual fruits of the International Eucharistic Congress to held in Cebu this coming January. We plan to organize a delegation for this Congress as a Jubilee of Mercy pilgrimage; next Sunday we will propose inscriptions for it. For more information on the Congress, please see its website
Special Collection
As an expression of the bond of unity and charity between our diocese and the Apostolic See, a second collection will be taken on June 27th-28th Masses. Cheques should be made payable to “The Catholic Diocese of Hong Kong”.
First Holy Communion
Formation for First Holy Communion will begin on September 13th. Children who are at least 7 years old and would like to join the formation should register in the sacristy as soon as possible.
Sunday School
If you might be interested in having Sunday school instruction separate from Service of the Word, please fill out the survey forms posted on the doors and bulletin boards.
8:30 Mass Coffee Morning
Due to a lack of volunteers, the coffee morning after 8:30 Mass will be held once a month, on the first Sunday. Please contact Marie Hendricks by email Chiliqueen58@gmail.com or phone (9033 2527) if you can help out.
Fr. Mitch
Rev. Mechelle Reginio, CICM, who until May was an assistant parish priest in St. Andrew’s Parish, has been appointed by our bishop to be an assistant parish priest in St. Jerome’s Parish in Tin Shui Wai from August 16th. Fr. Mitch thanks everyone warmly for the farewell party, cards, and gifts; he received a red priest’s stole made in Italy and a gift envelope with the remainder of the donations.
