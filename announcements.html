Announcements

Last update: 9-7-2016

July Pilgrimage
The bus for the pilgrimage will leave from St. Andrew’s in Hang Hau at 12:10pm this Sunday, July 10th. Please arrive no later than 12:00.
Adoration of the Blessed Sacrament
Our monthly Adoration will take place next Sunday, July 17th, at 3 pm and will be followed by a Biblical catechesis. Everyone is welcome. The Adoration is early this month as Fr. George will be going to World Youth Day in Krakow.
World Youth Day
If you ordered T-shirts to support World Youth Day, please confirm your order after Mass in the sacristy. The T-shirts will be distributed next Sunday, July 17th, after both Masses. The St. Andrew’s parish delegates to World Youth Day will also be sent off at the 6pm Mass at St. Andrew’s that Sunday. Everyone is welcome.
Ordinations
Our Bishop, Cardinal John Tong, cordially invites all members of the diocese to participate in the priestly ordination of our deacons Ignatius Lo King Ip and Paul Nguyen Kim Son on Saturday, August 20th at 3pm at the Cathedral of the Immaculate Conception. We are encouraged to pray for them and for new vocations to the priesthood and consecrated life.
