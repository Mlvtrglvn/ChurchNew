Announcements

Last update: 31-10-2014

Saturday Anticipated Mass
A three-month trial of a Saturday evening anticipated Mass will begin at St. Vincent’s on November 8th. In consultation with the church council, the time for Saturday Mass has been set at 5:30 pm.
Prayer for Departed
After 10:30 Mass on Sunday, November 2nd, a special prayer will be said for the departed priests, sisters and faithful of St. Vincent’s. A special basket will be on the offertory table during both Sunday Masses, and all are invited to put in photos of your deceased loved ones, which will be added to our memorial board, or written prayers for them.
All Saints’ Day
Mass will be celebrated for All Saints’ Day on Saturday, November 1st, at 5:30 pm.
All Souls Day
Religious services for the commemoration of all the faithful departed will take place at three Catholic cemeteries in Hong Kong on Sunday, November 2nd at 4 pm. More information about the times and locations of the services and about related plenary indulgences is posted on the bulletin board.
St. Andrew’s Novena and Adoration
In preparation for the celebration of the feast day of Saint Andrew, who is the patron saint of our parish, a special novena and Adoration of the Holy Sacrament will be held at St. Andrew’s Church in Hang Hau from Thursday, November 6th until Friday, November 14th every evening from 7:30 pm to 9:30 pm. It will also be possible to receive the sacrament of penance (confession). Everyone is invited to take part.
Mass for St. Andrew
The anticipated Mass for the Feast of St. Andrew will be held at our parish church of St. Andrew’s in Hang Hau on Sunday, November 16th, at 5 pm, with Fr. Joseph Ha Chi-Shing, OFM, auxiliary bishop of Hong Kong, presiding. Everyone is welcome to attend.
Sacraments for the Sick or Disabled
Parishioners who are ill or disabled and wish to regularly receive Holy Communion at home, request Confession or receive the Sacrament of the Anointing of the Sick, should please speak to Fr. George or one of the Extraordinary Ministers of Holy Communion before or after Mass.
Youth Choir
Young people and children who like to sing are invited to join a new Youth Choir being organized by Angelene Enguillo. If you might be interested in joining, please speak to Angelene, or to Ronnie Enguillo of the UFC-CWB.
Adoration of the Holy Sacrament
The Adoration of the Holy Sacrament will be held regularly on the afternoon of the last Sunday of each month.
Month of the Rosary
October is the Month of the Rosary, and the Sunday rosary at 10 am will be said especially for our families.
Photography group
A few volunteers are needed to take pictures of ceremonies and events at the church. If you might be interested in helping, please speak to Edith To at 8:30 Mass or Gilroy Fernandez at 10:30 Mass.
Flowers
Volunteers are very much needed to help with the flowers each week, and especially in the summer. Please consider taking a turn; there is a sign-up sheet on the bulletin board at the back of the church.
Reader Formation
Meetings will be held for all lectors and readers on November 16, November 30, December 14, and January 18 from 9:30 to 10:15 in the former parish office.
