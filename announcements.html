Announcements

Last update: 17-5-2014

Easter Cup 復活盃
The exercise group at St. Andrew’s is organizing an “Easter Cup” on Sunday, May 18th, from 2 to 4:30 pm at Catholic Ming Yuen Secondary School 天主教鳴遠中學, located on Hau Tak Estate 厚德村 in Hang Hau, a few minutes walk from the MTR. Activities will include 3-on-3 basketball, a sports area, and parent-child games. Everyone in the parish community is welcome.

World Day of Prayer for the Church in China
On Sunday, May 25th, we will celebrate the World Day of Prayer for the Church in China. Introduced by Pope Benedict XVI, this day is officially celebrated on May 24th in commemoration of Our Lady of Sheshan.
Church Planting Project
The Catholic Messengers of Green Consciousness invite us to join their “Church Planting Project”. A bilingual (English/Cantonese) “farmer” from the Catholic Messengers will hold two sessions from 12 noon to 1:00 pm on Sunday, 25 May and Sunday, 1 June on growing and monitoring the plants, which may be used for decorating the church or our own homes. Materials will be provided. If you are interested in participating, please sign up on the bulletin board, or contact Ellen (10:30 Mass) or Edith (8:30 Mass).

Adoration of the Holy Sacrament
The Adoration of the Holy Sacrament will be held regularly on the afternoon of the last Sunday of each month. Next adoration is scheduled on May 25th at 3.00 pm (the Hour of Divine Mercy).
Photography group
A few volunteers are needed to take pictures of ceremonies and events at the church. If you might be interested in helping, please speak to Edith To at 8:30 Mass or Gilroy Fernandez at 10:30 Mass.
International day
On Pentecost (June 8), we will celebrate the diversity of our congregation by having an International Day lunch and performances in the church hall after 10:30 Mass. Everyone is encouraged to participate by contributing a dish of food and by wearing your national costume or the colors of your country’s football team. We also invite performances—music, dance, poetry, or whatever talent you would like to share—please sign up on the sheet on the bulletin board at the back of the church.
Renovations of Church Hall
The preparations for renovation of the church hall downstairs have begun. We appreciate your patience with any inconvenience or disorder during the process of improvements to our building.
