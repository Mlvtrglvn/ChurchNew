Announcements

Last update: 9-3-2014

Lenten Theme
The theme of this year’s diocesan Lenten campaign from Ash Wednesday to Easter is “Faith comes to perfection with love”. Each week will also have a sub-theme to encourage us to participate through prayer, reflection and sacrifice.
Stations of the Cross
The Way of the Cross will be prayed at St. Vincent’s each Friday in Lent at 8 pm, beginning this coming Friday, March 7. A plenary indulgence is granted for those who piously complete this devotion. Confessions will also be heard before and after the meditation.
Holy Week Schedule
The schedule for Masses and other events for Holy Week is posted at the back of the church; leaflets are also available. It will be posted online soon.
Choir Representatives Meeting
There will be a meeting of the representatives of all the choirs on Sunday, March 16 at 2 pm. Anyone interested in music, even if not a member of one of the choirs, is also invited to attend.
