Announcements

Last update: 30-3-2015

Way of the Cross
The Stations of the Cross will be prayed at St. Vincent’s each Friday in Lent at 8 pm. Confessions will also be heard. A plenary indulgence will be granted to all who piously complete this devotion.
Special Collection
There will be a special collection on Good Friday at the celebration of the Passion of the Lord in support of our brethren in the Holy Land and the numerous pastoral, charitable, educational and social institutions and projects sponsored by the Church there. Any checks should be made out to “The Catholic Diocese of Hong Kong”.
Holy Week Schedule
Holy Thursday: The Mass of the Lord’s Supper will be held at 8 pm, followed by Adoration of the Holy Sacrament until 11 pm. Confessions will also be heard.

Good Friday: The Celebration of the Passion of the Lord will be at 3 pm. The Way of the Cross will be prayed at 6 pm. Please note that Good Friday is a day of fasting and abstinence from meat.

Holy Saturday: Easter Vigil Mass at 7:30 pm. Please note that there will be no 5:30 Mass this Saturday.

Easter Sunday: Mass at 8:30 and 10:30 am.

From Good Friday to Easter Sunday the Divine Mercy Novena will be led by the Legion of Mary; details are posted on the doors of the church. Schedule leaflets are available at the back of the church.
Easter Sunday
Easter Baskets: Families are invited to bring baskets with Easter foods for blessing at Mass. An explanation of this tradition and suggestions for basket preparation are posted at the back of the church.

Egg Hunt: There will be an egg hunt to the side of the church after both Masses on Easter Sunday. All children are welcome.

Cars: Please note that there will be no parking on the side of the church on Easter Sunday. We apologize for any inconvenience.
Shroud of Turin
A talk on the Shroud of Turin will be given by Fr. Andrew Dalton, L. C., at the chapel of Our Lady of Mount Carmel Church in Wanchai on Holy Thursday, April 2nd, from 5 to 6:30 pm. Mass will be at 8 pm.
8:30 Mass Coffee Morning
Due to a lack of volunteers, the coffee morning after 8:30 Mass will be held once a month, on the first Sunday. Please contact Marie Hendricks by email Chiliqueen58@gmail.com or phone (9033 2527) if you can help out.
Call for Readers (5:30 Mass)
Anyone willing to serve as a reader at 5:30 Mass, please speak to Fr. George or Ellen McGill after Mass.
