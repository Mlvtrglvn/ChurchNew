Announcements

Last update: 6-12-2014

Saturday Anticipated Mass
A three-month trial of a Saturday evening anticipated Mass will begin at St. Vincent’s on November 8th. In consultation with the church council, the time for Saturday Mass has been set at 5:30 pm.
8:30 Mass Service of the Word
Volunteers are needed to help with a Service of the Word for younger children at 8:30 Mass, which will begin on Sunday, November 30th. Service of the Word aims to present the scripture readings for that Sunday in an age-appropriate manner. Anyone interested please speak to Bridget Clancey at 8:30 Mass or Sr. Teresa.
Lector Formation
The fourth meeting of the formation for lectors will be held on Sunday, December 14th, from 9:30 to 10:15 in the former parish office. All candidates for lectorship are expected, and occasional readers encouraged, to attend.
Immaculate Conception
Mass will be celebrated at St. Vincent’s at 8 pm on Monday, December 8th for the Solemnity of the Immaculate Conception, followed by a Marian prayer as a sign of unity with a global chain of prayer in monasteries organized on this day by the Congregation for Institutes of Consecrated Life.
Simbang Gabi / Misa de Gallo
Mass will be said at St. Vincent’s every night at 9 pm from Monday, 15 December to Tuesday, 23 December. Everyone is welcomed to attend this novena leading up to Christmas.
Altar Servers
Children and young people who have received First Holy Communion are welcomed to become altar servers. Please come to the sacristy after Mass if interested.
Advent Action Bake Sale
We will have a bake sale on Sunday, 14th December, after both Masses. If you can donate items or help at the table, please sign up on the bulletin board.
Christmas Carols
Everyone is invited to gather for an evening of Christmas carols and potluck on Sunday, December 14th, from 6 to 8 pm at Spring Field Villas. The Turners and Reynolds have kindly agreed to host this St. Vincent’s tradition this year. Leaflets with directions and details are available at the back of the church.
Sacraments for the Sick or Disabled
Parishioners who are ill or disabled and wish to regularly receive Holy Communion at home, request Confession or receive the Sacrament of the Anointing of the Sick, should please speak to Fr. George or one of the Extraordinary Ministers of Holy Communion before or after Mass.
