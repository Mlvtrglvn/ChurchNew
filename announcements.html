Announcements

Last update: 24-4-2015

“Called and Gifted” Workshop
In cooperation with the Diocesan English-speaking Youth, the St. Francis Xavier Lay Missionary Society will hold a workshop on spiritual gifts at St. Anne’s Church in Stanley on April 30th and May 1st. Information is posted on the bulletin board; some scholarships are available.
Special Mass
On Sunday, Mary 3rd, at 11:30 am, a special Mass will be celebrated on Yim Tin Tsai (鹽田仔) Island at Saint Joseph’s Church in honor of the 125th anniversary of its consecration . All are cordially invited to attend the Mass and visit the village where Saint Joseph Freinademetz, SVD, lived and studied before serving as a missionary in Shandong. Boats to the island leave from Saikung Pier every 30 minutes.
Sunday School
If you might be interested in having Sunday school instruction separate from Service of the Word, please fill out the survey forms posted on the doors and bulletin boards.

Call for Readers (5:30 Mass)
Anyone willing to serve as a reader at 5:30 Mass, please speak to Fr. George or Ellen McGill after Mass.
