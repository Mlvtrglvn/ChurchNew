Announcements

Last update: 1-3-2015

Saturday Anticipated Mass
A three-month trial of a Saturday evening anticipated Mass will begin at St. Vincent’s on November 8th. In consultation with the church council, the time for Saturday Mass has been set at 5:30 pm.
Way of the Cross
The Stations of the Cross will be prayed at St. Vincent’s each Friday in Lent at 8 pm. Confessions will also be heard. A plenary indulgence will be granted to all who piously complete this devotion.
DEY Events
Information is posted on the bulletin board about several events in mid- March organized by the Diocesan English-speaking Youth group, including a bicycle pilgrimage and outdoor Way of the Cross.
24 Hours for the Lord
As part of this initiative, promoted by the Pontifical Council for the Promotion of the New Evangelization and the Holy Father, a special penitential liturgy will be held at St. Vincent’s on Friday, March 13th, along with the Way of the Cross; and Vespers will be recited after the 5:30 Mass on Saturday, March 14th. For more information on this initiative, please see http://www.novaevangelizatio.va/content/nvev/en/news/24ore2015.html and http://w2.vatican.va/content/francesco/en/messages/lent/documents/papa- francesco_20141004_messaggio-quaresima2015.html
8:30 Mass Coffee Morning
Due to a lack of volunteers, the coffee morning after 8:30 Mass will be held once a month, on the first Sunday. The next 8:30 coffee morning will be on March 1st. Please contact Marie Hendricks by email Chiliqueen58@gmail.com or phone (9033 2527) if you can help out.
Call for Readers (5:30 Mass)
Anyone willing to serve as a reader at 5:30 Mass, please speak to Fr. George or Ellen McGill after Mass.
