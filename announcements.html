Announcements

Last update: 18-5-2015

Pentecost/International Day
On Pentecost—May 24th—we will celebrate the diversity of our congregation by having an International Day lunch and performances in the church hall after 10:30 Mass. Please contribute a dish of food; wear your national costume or the colors of your country’s football team; share music, dance, poetry, or something else from your country. There are signup sheets on the bulletin board.
Parish Council Elections
The 13th St. Andrew’s Parish Council elections will be held at the parish annual general meeting on July 12th. Registration to be a candidate for the new council parish council, which will serve from 2015 to 2017, will be accepted after all anticipated and Sunday Masses at St. Andrew’s Church from Saturday, May 9th through Sunday, May 31st.
Corpus Christi Procession
Children, and especially those who received First Holy Communion this past November, are encouraged to take part in the procession to be held after the 10:30 Mass on June 7th,, the Feast of the Most Holy Body and Blood of Christ. A rehearsal will be held on Sunday, May 31st, after 10:30 Mass.
Blessing of Altar Servers
All altar servers and candidates are asked to join the blessing of altar servers to be held at the 8:30 and 10:30 Masses on June 7th.
Extraordinary Ministers Meeting
The annual meeting for all extraordinary ministers of the holy sacrament will be held after 10:30 Mass on June 7th.
Sunday School
If you might be interested in having Sunday school instruction separate from Service of the Word, please fill out the survey forms posted on the doors and bulletin boards.
8:30 Mass Coffee Morning
Due to a lack of volunteers, the coffee morning after 8:30 Mass will be held once a month, on the first Sunday. Please contact Marie Hendricks by email Chiliqueen58@gmail.com or phone (9033 2527) if you can help out.
Call for Readers (5:30 Mass)
Anyone willing to serve as a reader at 5:30 Mass, please speak to Fr. George or Ellen McGill after Mass.
