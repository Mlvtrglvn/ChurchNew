Holy Week Schedule
  • In English at St. Vincent's church

Lenten Theme
The theme of this year’s diocesan Lenten campaign from Ash Wednesday to Easter is “Faith comes to perfection with love”. Each week will also have a sub-theme to encourage us to participate through prayer, reflection and sacrifice.
Stations of the Cross
The Way of the Cross will be prayed at St. Vincent’s each Friday in Lent at 8 pm. A plenary indulgence is granted for those who piously complete this devotion. Confessions will also be heard before and after the meditation.
Adoration of the Holy Sacrament
All are invited to attend the Adoration of the Holy Sacrament on Sunday, March 30, at 3:30 pm. The Adoration will be held regularly on the afternoon of the last Sunday of each month.
Renovations of Church Hall
The preparations for renovation of the church hall downstairs have begun. We appreciate your patience with any inconvenience or disorder during the process of improvements to our building.
Presentation on Labour and Immigration Laws: Sun., March 23, 1-5 pm
On Sunday, March 23 from 1 to 5 pm the Overseas Workers Welfare Administration of the Philippines Consulate and the organization Filcomsin (Filipino Community Services and Information Network) will make a presentation at St. Vincent’s to update our community on Hong Kong labour and immigration laws. Everyone is invited to attend.
Enrollment for Confirmation of Young People and Rite of the Christian Initiation for Adults
Until April 27, applications will be accepted for young people 14 and older who wish to be confirmed and for adults interested in the Rite of the Christian Initiation for Adults. Enrollment forms and more information may be obtained in the sacristy. Applications for First Holy Communion will be accepted after Easter.
Charity Sale for Caritas
A sale of housewares, electronics and other items will be held on Sunday, March 30, downstairs outside the church hall after both Masses. Proceeds will go to support the Lenten collection for Caritas. Please note that there will be NO PARKING outside the church hall due to this event.
