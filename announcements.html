Announcements

Last update: 1-2-2015

Saturday Anticipated Mass
A three-month trial of a Saturday evening anticipated Mass will begin at St. Vincent’s on November 8th. In consultation with the church council, the time for Saturday Mass has been set at 5:30 pm.
Music festival
Thank you very much to all those who made last Sunday’s Music Festival a joyful occasion, by organizing the event, sharing your talents, donating food—the bake sales raised over $2000 towards Huiling—or being an appreciative audience.
Jumble Sale
On Sunday, 8 February, we will have a jumble sale after both 8:30 and 10:30 Masses downstairs in the church hall to raise funds for Huiling. If you can donate items—clothes, toys, books, appliances—please contact Marie Hendricks by email Chiliqueen58@gmail.com or phone (9033 2527); or bring them to Mass.
World Day of the Sick
The twenty-third World Day of the Sick, and the commemorative feast of Our Lady of Lourdes, falls on February 11th. In this context, at the anticipated and Sunday Masses next week we will pray for all who are burdened by illness, as well as for all professionals and volunteers in the field of health care. This year’s theme invites us to reflect on a phrase from the Book of Job: “I was eyes to the blind, and feet to the lame” (Job 29:15). If any parishioner who is ill wishes to receive the Anointing of the Sick or other sacraments please inform Fr. George or Sr. Teresa.
Confirmation
Confirmation classes will begin on February 22, the first Sunday of Lent. If you would like to be confirmed, please speak with Fr. George.
