Announcements

Last update: 24-10-2015

All Saints’ / All Souls’ Days
On Sunday, November 1st (the Solemnity of All Saints’ and Vigil of All Souls’ Day), we will celebrate the solemn Vespers (evening prayer) at 5:30 pm. It will be concluded by the occasional prayers for our departed parishioners, family members and friends. Please bring their pictures with you to put on our Memorial. On All Souls’ Day (Monday, November 2 nd ) a special Mass will be celebrated in Saint Andrew’s Church at 8pm. Religious services for the commemoration of all the faithful departed will also take place on All Souls’ Day at 4pm at three Catholic cemeteries (Happy Valley, Chai Wan, and Cheung Sha Wan). Please see the chancery notice on the bulletin board for more information.
Raffle Tickets for Charity
From the Feast of Saint Vincent, who is the patron of charity, we will raise funds for Caritas, which provides various services to the Hong Kong community (including education, social work, and medical services). Raffle tickets will be sold after Mass for $20 each on a first come first serve basis. The drawing will take place on 8 November at Caritas Kowloon Bazaar.
Sunday School
Catechesis for children and young people will begin in October. Pre-Communion, post-Communion (as preparation for the renewal of the Profession of Faith), pre-Confirmation, and post-Confirmation (on Catholic ethics and moral values) classes are being organized. Please register outside the church.
Coffee Morning
Coffee morning after 10:30 Mass each week, and on the first Sunday of the month after 8:30 Mass, provide a chance for parishioners to get to know each other and share fellowship. If you enjoy these occasions, please take a turn to provide and serve snacks and drinks. Sign up sheets are on the bulletin board. Thank you!
Plenary Indulgence
A plenary indulgence, applicable only to the souls in Purgatory, is granted to the faithful who devoutly visit a cemetery between November 1 st and November 8 th and pray for the departed; or who devoutly visit a church or oratory on November 2 nd and recite the Our Father and the Creed. Please see the chancery notice on the bulletin board for details.
World Youth Day
With the consent of our bishop, St. Andrew’s and Tai Po parishes will together organize one of the diocesan groups to participate in World Youth Day in Krakow (Poland) in July 2016. Only 20 young people (16-35 years old) from each parish are permitted and will include both English- and Chinese-speaking youth. The deadline for registration is October 18th (Mission Sunday); enrollment forms and detailed information are available in the sacristy. Father Fabio and Father George will be in charge of our parish group.

Blessing of Rosaries
October is traditionally the month of the Holy Rosary. Everyone, but especially First Communion and Confirmation candidates, are encouraged to bring a rosary to be blessed next Sunday. Please leave the rosaries in the sacristy before 10:30 Mass.
