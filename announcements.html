Announcements

Last update: 25-1-2015

Saturday Anticipated Mass
A three-month trial of a Saturday evening anticipated Mass will begin at St. Vincent’s on November 8th. In consultation with the church council, the time for Saturday Mass has been set at 5:30 pm.
Special Collection
We will have the monthly special collection for Advent Action at all Masses on January 31st - February 1st weekend. Any checks should be made out to “Hong Kong Huiling Ltd”; if you would like a receipt for a donation of HK$100 or more, please enclose one of the receipt requests forms available on the bulletin boards.
Jumble Sale
On Sunday, 8 February, we will have a jumble sale after both 8:30 and 10:30 Masses downstairs in the church hall to raise funds for Huiling. If you can donate items—clothes, toys, books, appliances—please contact Marie Hendricks by email Chiliqueen58@gmail.com or phone (9033 2527); or bring them to Mass.
Day of Prayer for the Consecrated Life
On Monday, February 2nd, which is the Feast of the Presentation of the Lord or Candlemas, and also the diocesan Day of Prayer for the Consecrated Life, Saint Ignatius Chapel on Waterloo Road near the Yau Ma Tei MTR will hold an Adoration of the Holy Sacrament at 4 pm, followed by Mass at 5 pm. All the faithful are cordially invited.
Confirmation
Confirmation classes will begin on February 22, the first Sunday of Lent. If you would like to be confirmed, please speak with Fr. George.
Theology of Family
A course on the Theology of Family is being organized by the Saint John Paul II Formation Centre in Kennedy Town from January 26th to February 1st. Please see the poster on the large bulletin board for more information.
Flowers
Volunteers are very much needed to help with flowers; you can be reimbursed up to $400 if you provide a receipt. Please sign up on the list posted on the small bulletin board at the back of the church.
