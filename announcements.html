Announcements

Last update: 22-11-2015

Adoration of the Blessed Sacrament
Our monthly Adoration will be held on Sunday, November 22 nd , at 3 pm. We will pray for our good preparation of the Jubilee of Mercy. It will be followed by a short Biblical catechesis on the meaning of the jubilee year in the Old Testament. Everyone is welcome to attend.
Sunday School
Catechesis for children and young people will begin in October. Pre-Communion, post-Communion (as preparation for the renewal of the Profession of Faith), pre-Confirmation, and post-Confirmation (on Catholic ethics and moral values) classes are being organized. Please register outside the church.
Coffee Morning
Coffee morning after 10:30 Mass each week, and on the first Sunday of the month after 8:30 Mass, provide a chance for parishioners to get to know each other and share fellowship. If you enjoy these occasions, please take a turn to provide and serve snacks and drinks. Sign up sheets are on the bulletin board. Thank you!
Advent Action: Caritas-Nepal
Each winter, in honor of our patron saint, the St. Vincent’s community raises funds from the beginning of Advent through Chinese New Year for a charity that serves the very poor or marginalized. This year we will support the earthquake relief and community rebuilding organized by Caritas—Nepal. More information about the charity and the progress of Advent Action will be available on the bulletin board. Anyone interested in helping is very welcome—please speak to any member of the pastoral team or church council to offer ideas or time. Your generosity allows us each year to share some of the joy of the Advent, Christmas and New Year seasons with those less fortunate. To begin this year’s Advent Action:
  • Bake Sale: On Sunday, November 29 th , at St. Vincent’s we will have a Bake Sale and a chance to “Guess the Weight” of a homemade Christmas fruitcake after both Masses. Closest guess wins the cake! Donations of food to sell are very much needed; you can drop them off before Mass at the tables outside. Please speak to Marie Hendricks or Lani Nasayao if you have questions.
  • Toy Sale: There will be a sale of new toys next Saturday evening and Sunday (November 28 th and 29 th ) until 1 pm at our parish church of St. Andrew in Hang Hau. Please stop by!
Immaculate Conception
Mass will be celebrated at St. Vincent’s on Tuesday, December 8, at 8 pm for the Solemnity of the Immaculate Conception and the beginning of the Jubilee Year of Mercy. On Saturday, December 12th, at 6 pm, the Opening of the Holy Door of Jubilee and the commissioning of servers at seven pilgrimage churches will be celebrated at Cathedral of the Immaculate Conception with Cardinal John Tong officiating.
