Youth and Reader Formation
On this Sunday, February 28th we will begin
  • 9:45 am: Post-Communion catechesis: For young people who have received First Communion but not yet been confirmed
  • 11:30 am: Post-confirmation / reader catechesis: For young people who have already been confirmed, as well as anyone who would like to become a reader.
To be a reader, you must have been confirmed. The reader-related part of the formation will last until June. All meetings will be in the old parish office. If you have not yet registered for catechesis, or would like to attend in order to become a reader, please speak with Fr. George.
24 Hours for the Lord
We will join in one of Pope Francis’ initiatives for the Jubilee Year of Mercy entitled "24 hours for the Lord". We will begin on Friday, March 4th at 8pm with the Stations of the Cross followed by Mass and Adoration of the Holy Sacrament until 11pm. During the Adoration it will be possible to receive the sacrament of reconciliation or spiritual guidance. Adoration and confessions will continue on Saturday, March 5th, from 10 am to 5:30pm.
Extraordinary Minister Formation
Meetings for those who would like to become extraordinary ministers of Holy Communion will begin on Sunday, March 13. The deadline to register is March 6.
Advent Action
A total of HK$67,250 was raised for the earthquake relief and community rebuilding being undertaken in Nuwakot District by Caritas—Nepal. The funds have been deposited with Caritas; receipts for those who requested them are in process. Thank you very much to all of you for your generous donations of both money and time to the 2015 Advent Action.
Pilgrimage
The pastoral team and church council would like to express their gratitude to those who helped organize the pilgrimage to St. Joseph’s Chapel on Yim Tin Tsai last weekend.
Stations of the Cross
The Way of the Cross will be prayed at St. Vincent’s every Friday evening in Lent at 8 pm. A plenary indulgence is granted for those who piously complete this devotion. Confessions will also be heard before and after the meditation.
Church Council
The membership of the 2016 church council has been posted on the doors and bulletin board. Please join us in thanking the outgoing council members for their hard work and devotion over the last year, and in warmly welcoming the new council.
