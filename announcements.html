Announcements

Last update: 9-7-2016

Confirmation classes
On the first Sunday of Advent (November 27th), we will begin our classes for the sacrament of confirmation. All candidates should be 12 years old or above and should present their certificate of baptism. We invite parents of our candidates to fill the application form in our sacristy. The deadline is on Sunday, November 20th, and the tentative confirmation date will be on Saturday, June 3rd next year.
Conclusion of the Jubilee Year of Mercy
Before the official conclusion of the Jubilee Year of Mercy by Pope Francis in Rome next Sunday, November 20th, our bishops invite us to attend the closure of the Year of Mercy in any of the seven jubilee churches in Hong Kong.
