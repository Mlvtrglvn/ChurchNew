Announcements

Last update: 15-2-2016

Books / Clothes Sale
Thanks very much to all those who donated items to or helped organize the sale of books and clothes, as well as to those who bought items. $3560 was raised by the sale for Caritas-Nepal.
Pilgrimage
This Sunday is the deadline to register for the pilgrimage to the Jubilee church of St. Joseph’s on Yim Tin Tsai 鹽田仔 island in Sai Kung on Sunday, February 21. Please sign up on the bulletin board.
We will meet at the Sai Kung pier at 10:15 am; the boat leaves at 10:30. Mass will be celebrated at St. Joseph’s chapel; please bring a picnic lunch. The pilgrimage will end about 2:30pm. Tickets will be $50/person to cover the roundtrip cost of the boat and a donation to St. Joseph’s.
Stations of the Cross
The Way of the Cross will be prayed at St. Vincent’s every Friday evening in Lent at 8 pm, beginning this Friday.
Coffee Morning
Coffee morning after 10:30 Mass each week, and on the first Sunday of the month after 8:30 Mass, provide a chance for parishioners to get to know each other and share fellowship. If you enjoy these occasions, please take a turn to provide and serve snacks and drinks. Sign up sheets are on the bulletin board. Thank you!
