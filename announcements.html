Announcements

Last update: 21-6-2015

First Holy Communion
Formation for First Holy Communion will begin on September 13th. Children who are at least 7 years old and would like to join the formation should register in the sacristy.
Farewell / Welcome Party
Saint Andrew`s parish will organize a special farewell for Fr. Pasini and a welcoming party for our new priests, Fr. Benedict Lam 林祖明神父 and Fr. Fabio Favata 林柏榮神父, on Saturday, August 22nd, from 3 to 5 pm, in Catholic Ming Yuen Secondary School 天主教鳴遠中學, Hau Tak Estate, Hang Hau. Tickets are $50. Please speak with Sister Teresa before Sunday, August 9th if you would like to participate.
Diocesan Youth Day 2015
Diocesan Youth Day will be celebrated at Wukaisha Youth Village from Friday, August 14th at 5 pm through Sunday, August 16th. Mass will be celebrated on Sunday from 2 to 4 pm. Information about registration fees is posted on the bulletin board. The event is aimed at Catholics and catechumens between 12 and 35 years old. It will be mainly in Cantonese but all are welcome to attend.
Rite of Christian Initiation of Adults
The first meeting for new catechumens and those interested in receiving the sacraments of Christian initiation will be held on Sunday, August 30th, after 10:30 Mass. Please inform family members, friends, students and others who could be interested.
Sunday School
If you might be interested in having Sunday school instruction separate from Service of the Word, please fill out the survey forms posted on the doors and bulletin boards.
