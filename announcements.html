Announcements

Last update: 6-2-2015

Saturday Anticipated Mass
A three-month trial of a Saturday evening anticipated Mass will begin at St. Vincent’s on November 8th. In consultation with the church council, the time for Saturday Mass has been set at 5:30 pm.
Advent Action Special Collection
We will have the final special collection for Advent Action at all Masses next weekend. Any checks should be made out to “Hong Kong Huiling Ltd”. Receipt request forms for donations of HK$100 or more are available on both bulletin boards, as is more information about the charity Huiling.
Jumble Sale
On Sunday, 8 February, we will have a jumble sale after both 8:30 and 10:30 Masses downstairs in the church hall to raise funds for Huiling. If you can donate items—clothes, toys, books, appliances—please contact Marie Hendricks by email Chiliqueen58@gmail.com or phone (9033 2527); or bring them to Mass.
Anticipated Celebration of World Day for the Sick
On Sunday, February 8th, activities in honor of the World Day of the Sick will be held from 1 to 7 pm at Caritas Hall on Caine Road. These include Mass at 3:30 pm and an Anointing of the Sick, both of which will be celebrated by Michael Yeung, Auxiliary Bishop of Hong Kong.
Confirmation
Confirmation classes will begin on February 22, the first Sunday of Lent. If you would like to be confirmed, please speak with Fr. George.
8:30 Mass Coffee Morning
Due to a lack of volunteers, the coffee morning after 8:30 Mass will be held once a month, on the first Sunday. The next 8:30 coffee morning will be on March 1st. Please contact Marie Hendricks by email Chiliqueen58@gmail.com or phone (9033 2527) if you can help out.
Palms Collection
Please bring old branches and palms blessed on Palm Sunday last year to the sacristy by Monday, February 16th. They will be burned that Tuesday to make ashes for Ash Wednesday.
Ash Wednesday
Mass will be celebrated and ashes distributed on the evening of Ash Wednesday, February 18th, at 8 pm. Our diocese directs that, as Ash Wednesday coincides with the Lunar New Year Eve, the faithful are dispensed from fast and abstinence on that day, but are obliged to choose some other suitable penance or works of charity, in keeping with the penitential spirit of the season of Lent. The rite of the giving of ashes will also be held at 8 pm on Friday, February 27th, during the Way of the Cross. In accordance with liturgical tradition and diocesan direction, this rite should not be held on a Sunday.
Chinese New Year Mass
Mass will be celebrated at St. Vincent’s at 8:30 am on Thursday, February 19th, the first morning of the Chinese New Year.
Stations of the Cross
The Way of the Cross will be prayed at St. Vincent’s each Friday in Lent at 8 pm, beginning on February 18th. A plenary indulgence is granted for those who piously complete this devotion. Confessions will also be heard before and after the meditation.
Call for Readers (5:30 Mass)
Anyone willing to serve as a reader at 5:30 Mass, please speak to Fr. George or Ellen McGill after Mass.
