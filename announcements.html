Announcements

Last update: 6-6-2014

International day
On Pentecost (June 8), four parishioners will receive the sacrament of Confirmation at 10:30 Mass. Afterwards we will celebrate the diversity of our congregation by having an International Day lunch and performances in the church hall after 10:30 Mass.
  • Volunteers are needed to help set up and decorate the hall on Saturday, 7 June, at noon.
  • For the lunch, please contribute a dish of food; and consider wearing your national costume or the colors of your country’s football team; or sharing music, dance, poetry, or something else from your country (music, dance, poetry).
  • To reduce waste, please bring your own plates and cutlery to the lunch; there will be a charge for any disposable plates or cutlery provided by the church.
Tian’anmen Memorial Events in the Diocese
  • Sunday, June 1: The Catholic Concern Groups will meet at 2:40 pm (in front of the Queen Victoria Statue) and pray before joining the Pro-Democracy March.
  • Wednesday, June 4: There will be a prayer meeting from 6:45 to 7:30 pm at the Victoria Park bandstand before the candlelight vigil in memory of Tian’anmen.
  • More Information: For information (in Chinese only) on other activities in our diocese in memory of June 4th, please see the webpage of the Justice & Peace Commission of the Hong Kong Catholic Diocese.
  • Sunday, June 8: The social concern group of St. Andrew's together with the Diocesan Justice and Peace Commission will organise a seminar on "The Meaning of Casting Your Vote” at 3 pm.
Joyful Feast 2014
The Joyful Feast 2014—Touch International Youth Festival will be held this year at the Chinese University of Hong Kong from July 18 to July 20. Aimed at Catholics between the ages of 15 to 25, it is also open to people of different ages and faiths; the program is conducted in Cantonese, Mandarin and English. For more information, please see their website
Coffee Morning Volunteers
Sharing snacks and drinks after Mass helps build the St. Vincent’s community and is one of the small ways we serve other community members. Volunteers are very much needed; if you enjoy this tradition, please consider taking a turn. For 8:30 Mass, speak to Edith To; for 10:30 Mass, to Marie Hendricks or Lani Nasayao, or sign up on the bulletin board.
Luggage removal
We greatly appreciate how quickly people responded to the appeal to remove personal items from the church hall and school. We would be grateful if in future storage of personal items, as well as attaching anything to the walls, could be avoided. Thank you very much for your cooperation.
Adoration of the Holy Sacrament
The Adoration of the Holy Sacrament will be held regularly on the afternoon of the last Sunday of each month.
Rosary
After May, we will continue to pray the rosary each Sunday from 10:00 am to 10:20 am. Everyone is invited to join.
Photography group
A few volunteers are needed to take pictures of ceremonies and events at the church. If you might be interested in helping, please speak to Edith To at 8:30 Mass or Gilroy Fernandez at 10:30 Mass.
Institution of Extraordinary Ministers
On Corpus Christi (June 22) the rite of the Institution of Extraordinary Ministers of the Holy Sacrament will be held after 10:30 Mass. As part of this ceremony, children, and especially those who received First Holy Communion last year or will receive it this year, are invited to join the procession of the Holy Sacrament after Mass.
Renovations of Church Hall
The preparations for renovation of the church hall downstairs have begun. We appreciate your patience with any inconvenience or disorder during the process of improvements to our building.
