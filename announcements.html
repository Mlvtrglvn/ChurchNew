Announcements

Last update: 15-6-2014

Corpus Christi
On Corpus Christi (June 22) the rite of the Institution of Extraordinary Ministers of the Holy Sacrament will be held at 10:30 Mass. After both Masses, there will be a short procession with the Holy Sacrament. Children, and especially those who received First Holy Communion last year or will receive it this year, are invited to join the Procession; they are encouraged to wear white clothing and to bring baskets of flower petals and leaves to scatter during the procession. Members of the choirs and Legion of Mary are also invited to wear their uniforms.
Joyful Feast 2014
The Joyful Feast 2014—Touch International Youth Festival will be held this year at the Chinese University of Hong Kong from July 18 to July 20. Aimed at Catholics between the ages of 15 to 25, it is also open to people of different ages and faiths; the program is conducted in Cantonese, Mandarin and English. For more information, please see their website
Coffee Morning
Sharing snacks and drinks after Mass helps build the St. Vincent’s community and is one of the small ways we serve other community members. To run it continuously relies on your support and contributions of food and labour.
  • 8:30 Mass : Due to the shortage of participating families to run coffee morning, in the summer, it will be suspended at 8:30 Mass until September. Anyone who would be interested in helping out in the fall, please contact Edith To.
  • 10:30 Mass: Volunteers are very much needed; if you enjoy this tradition, please consider taking a turn. Information and a sign up sheet are posted on the bulletin board; or speak Lani Nasayao.
Adoration of the Holy Sacrament
The Adoration of the Holy Sacrament will be held regularly on the afternoon of the last Sunday of each month.
Rosary
After May, we will continue to pray the rosary each Sunday from 10:00 am to 10:20 am. Everyone is invited to join.
Photography group
A few volunteers are needed to take pictures of ceremonies and events at the church. If you might be interested in helping, please speak to Edith To at 8:30 Mass or Gilroy Fernandez at 10:30 Mass.
Pentecost / International day
We would like to express our gratitude to everyone who helped with the Pentecost/International Day celebrations last Sunday, whether by contributing food or performances, setting up and cleaning up, or taking on the immense task of cleaning and decorating the church and church hall prior to the event. Thank you very much for your enthusiasm for and devotion to the St. Vincent’s community.
Renovations of Church Hall
The preparations for renovation of the church hall downstairs have begun. We appreciate your patience with any inconvenience or disorder during the process of improvements to our building.
