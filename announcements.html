Announcements

Last update: 18-7-2014

Episcopal Ordination
The episcopal ordination of the three Auxiliary Bishops-elect of our Diocese, namely, the Most Rev. Michael YEUNG Ming-cheung, Stephen LEE Bun-sang and Joseph HA Chi-shing, OFM, will be held in the Catholic Cathedral, Caine Road, on Saturday, 30 August 2014, at 3:00 p.m., with Cardinal John TONG presiding. The faithful and the clergy are welcome to participate.
Joyful Feast 2014
The Joyful Feast 2014—Touch International Youth Festival will be held this year at the Chinese University of Hong Kong from July 18 to July 20. Aimed at Catholics between the ages of 15 to 25, it is also open to people of different ages and faiths; the program is conducted in Cantonese, Mandarin and English. For more information, please see their website .
Hong Kong Book Fair
Information about the Hong Kong Book Fair, which runs until July 22nd, is posted on the doors and bulletin board. The Fair includes a special section on religion with stalls from a number of Catholic presses.
Adoration of the Holy Sacrament
The Adoration of the Holy Sacrament will be held regularly on the afternoon of the last Sunday of each month. We will pray particularly for the fruitful first apostolic travel of the Pope in Asia and for the Hong Kong delegation to Asian Youth Day.
Rosary
After May, we will continue to pray the rosary each Sunday from 10:00 am to 10:20 am. Everyone is invited to join.
Photography group
A few volunteers are needed to take pictures of ceremonies and events at the church. If you might be interested in helping, please speak to Edith To at 8:30 Mass or Gilroy Fernandez at 10:30 Mass.
Legion of Mary Anniversary
The two Legion of Mary groups, Mother of Divine Grace and Mary, Help of Christians, are celebrating their 26th and 2nd anniversaries respectively. On behalf of the officers and members, I would like to express our heartfelt thanks to everyone for journeying with us and helping us grow spiritually while serving here in St. Vincent’s Church. May the Good Lord bless us all.
