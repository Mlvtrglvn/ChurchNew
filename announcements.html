Service and Event Schedule
Schedules of the Advent and Christmas Services and Events at both St. Andrew’s and St. Vincent’s are available at the back of the church.

    • Confession: 6pm-8 pm; 10pm-11pm

    • Announcement of Nativity and Caroling: 11:15 pm
    • Midnight Mass: 12 am
  • Friday, December 25th:
    • Christmas Mass: 8:30 am and 10:30 am
Christmas Celebration
On Sunday, December 27th, there will be a potluck lunch with some carols and games downstairs in the church hall after 10:30 Mass. Everyone is welcome; please bring some food to share. If you would like to sing or otherwise perform, please speak with Ronnie Enguillo or Raul Labicane.
Simbang Gabi / Misa de Gallo
Mass will be said at St. Vincent’s every night at 9 pm from Tuesday, 15 December to Wednesday, 23 December. Everyone is welcomed to attend this novena leading up to Christmas.
Adoration and Catechesis
All are invited to attend the Adoration of the Holy Sacrament on Sunday, December 27th, at 3:00 pm, followed by a short Biblical catechesis.
Jubilee Year of Mercy
The opening of the Holy Door in St. Joseph’s Chapel (Yim Tin Tsai Village, Sai Kung) and four other Jubilee Churches will take place simultaneously on Friday, January 1st (the Solemnity of Mary, Mother of God) at 3 pm. During this year our parish will organize several pilgrimages to these seven Jubilee Churches. For details, please see the information at the back of the church.
World Day of Peace
The 49th World Day of Peace will be celebrated on Friday, January 1st; the main theme is: “Overcome Indifference and Win Peace”. In response to the Holy Father’s appeal to promote world peace, Cardinal John TONG will preside at a special concelebrated Mass at the Cathedral at 10:00 am. Both clergy and laity are cordially invited to participate.
Coffee Morning
Coffee morning after 10:30 Mass each week, and on the first Sunday of the month after 8:30 Mass, provide a chance for parishioners to get to know each other and share fellowship. If you enjoy these occasions, please take a turn to provide and serve snacks and drinks. Sign up sheets are on the bulletin board. Thank you!
Advent Action: Caritas-Nepal
Each winter, in honor of our patron saint, the St. Vincent’s community raises funds from the beginning of Advent through Chinese New Year for a charity that serves the very poor or marginalized. This year we will support the earthquake relief and community rebuilding organized by Caritas—Nepal. More information about the charity and the progress of Advent Action will be available on the bulletin board. Anyone interested in helping is very welcome—please speak to any member of the pastoral team or church council to offer ideas or time. Your generosity allows us each year to share some of the joy of the Advent, Christmas and New Year seasons with those less fortunate. To begin this year’s Advent Action:
  • Bake Sale: On Sunday, November 29 th , at St. Vincent’s we will have a Bake Sale and a chance to “Guess the Weight” of a homemade Christmas fruitcake after both Masses. Closest guess wins the cake! Donations of food to sell are very much needed; you can drop them off before Mass at the tables outside. Please speak to Marie Hendricks or Lani Nasayao if you have questions.
  • Toy Sale: There will be a sale of new toys next Saturday evening and Sunday (November 28 th and 29 th ) until 1 pm at our parish church of St. Andrew in Hang Hau. Please stop by!
