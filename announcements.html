Announcements

Last update: 18-12-2014

Saturday Anticipated Mass
A three-month trial of a Saturday evening anticipated Mass will begin at St. Vincent’s on November 8th. In consultation with the church council, the time for Saturday Mass has been set at 5:30 pm.
8:30 Mass Service of the Word
Volunteers are needed to help with a Service of the Word for younger children at 8:30 Mass, which will begin on Sunday, November 30th. Service of the Word aims to present the scripture readings for that Sunday in an age-appropriate manner. Anyone interested please speak to Bridget Clancey at 8:30 Mass or Sr. Teresa.
Advent/Christmas Services and Events
Schedules for services and events during Advent and Christmas at both St. Vincent’s and St. Andrew’s are available at the back of the church. The following are at St. Vincent’s unless otherwise noted:
  • Simbang Gabi / Misa de Gallo:Mass at 9 pm every night until Tuesday, December 23
  • Christmas Eve:
    • Confession:6 – 8 pm; 10 – 11 pm
    • Christmas Eve Mass:8 pm
    • Midnight Mass:from 11:15 pm
  • Christmas:Mass at 8:30 and 10:30
  • December 26: Christmas children’s games from 1:30 pm to 3:30 pm at St. Andrew’s Church in Hang Hau
  • New Year’s Eve:10:30 pm Thanksgiving Mass, followed by Adoration
  • New Year’s Day:Solemnity of the Mother of God Mass at 10:30 am
Altar Servers
There is a meeting for all altar servers and candidates to become altar servers on Sunday, December 21 after 10:30 Mass in the sacristy.
Adoration and Catechesis
All are invited to attend the Adoration of the Holy Sacrament on Sunday, December 28th, at 3:00 pm; as it is the Feast of the Holy Family, we will pray for our families. Biblical catechesis will begin about 4 pm.
Confirmation
Confirmation classes will begin on February 22, the first Sunday of Lent. If you would like to be confirmed, please speak with Fr. George.
Bake Sale and Caroling
We are very grateful to all those who took the time to help with the bake sale last week, whether by donating food, working at the table, or purchasing items; the sale raised just over $4500 for Huiling. We would also like to express appreciation to everyone who attended the caroling last Sunday night and made it a joyful occasion, especially those who led the music and hosted the event.
Conduct in Church
A gentle reminder to treat the church as a sacred space: please avoid eating or drinking inside the church or disturbing those who wish to pray before or after Mass.
Events for Youth
Information about several events for young people both in Hong Kong and the Philippines is posted on the bulletin board.
