Announcements

Last update: 27-6-2014

Special Collection for the Apostolic See
As an expression of the bond of unity and charity between our diocese and the Apostolic See, a second collection will be taken up at all public Masses on Sunday, June 29. Any cheques should be made payable to “The Catholic Diocese of Hong Kong.”
Joyful Feast 2014
The Joyful Feast 2014—Touch International Youth Festival will be held this year at the Chinese University of Hong Kong from July 18 to July 20. Aimed at Catholics between the ages of 15 to 25, it is also open to people of different ages and faiths; the program is conducted in Cantonese, Mandarin and English. For more information, please see their website .
6.22 Civil Referendum 6.22 民間全民投票
Assistance with online voting will be provided at St. Andrew’s today before and after each Mass for about half an hour for those who wish to participate in PopVote. Also, St. Andrew’s will be one of the new Tseung Kwan O voting stations on Sunday, June 29. Anyone interested in volunteering to help on that day, please contact Edith To.
Coffee Morning
Sharing snacks and drinks after Mass helps build the St. Vincent’s community and is one of the small ways we serve other community members. To run it continuously relies on your support and contributions of food and labour.
  • 8:30 Mass : Due to the shortage of participating families to run coffee morning, in the summer, it will be suspended at 8:30 Mass until September. Anyone who would be interested in helping out in the fall, please contact Edith To.
  • 10:30 Mass: Volunteers are very much needed; if you enjoy this tradition, please consider taking a turn. Information and a sign up sheet are posted on the bulletin board; or speak Lani Nasayao.
Adoration of the Holy Sacrament
The Adoration of the Holy Sacrament will be held regularly on the afternoon of the last Sunday of each month. During this "month of the Sacred Heart" we will renew the annual consecration of our families to the Most Sacred Heart of Jesus.
Rosary
After May, we will continue to pray the rosary each Sunday from 10:00 am to 10:20 am. Everyone is invited to join.
Photography group
A few volunteers are needed to take pictures of ceremonies and events at the church. If you might be interested in helping, please speak to Edith To at 8:30 Mass or Gilroy Fernandez at 10:30 Mass.
Thank you!
We are very grateful to everyone who helped with the celebration of Corpus Christi last Sunday: liturgy, readings, songs, decorations, and flower-scattering. Your enthusiasm and energy are much appreciated.
